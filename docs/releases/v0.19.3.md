# v0.19.3 — fix: stale sync-lock rows after unclean shutdown

Third and (hopefully) final patch in the maria UAT rollout series ([v0.19.2](v0.19.2.md)).

### Fixes

- **KB syncs fail "Another sync is in progress" after a pod restart.** gbrain's sync/dream advisory locks live as rows in `gbrain_cycle_locks` (host+pid attributed, 30-minute TTL). A pod killed mid-sync leaves its row on the PVC; the replacement pod has a different hostname, so gbrain's cross-host policy refuses to steal the lock until TTL expiry — up to 30 minutes of failing KB syncs on every unclean restart. Boot now sweeps the table: at fresh-process start slaude owns the brain exclusively (one container = one persona), so any surviving lock row is stale by construction. `SLAUDE_BRAIN_TAKEOVER=0` opts out of both this and the v0.19.2 PGLite-lock takeover. (`src/knowledge/brain.ts`)
